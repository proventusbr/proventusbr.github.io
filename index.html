About

Proventus and Thiago Camargo

  • 2002-2004

    OpenSource Softphones

    VoIP was brewing its popuplarity, the lack of plug and play software was an opportunity for Proventus to start and fill the gap.

  • 2004-2005

    Voice Over IP made in Brazil

    Proventus started to provide professional services on IP Telephony Desktop Clients and Servers for Telecom and small businesses in Brazil

  • 2006-2007

    VoIP Overseas

    VoIP Market became more mature and Proventus starts to provide international services and conduct researches for US and Europe Companies

  • 2008-2009

    Mobile VoIP

    Mobile Phone became more powerfull bringing VoIP capabilities. Proventus build the first XMPP Jingle Based Mobile Platform.

  • 2010-2011

    Jingle Nodes and P2P

    With the boom of Mobile Users, massive scale was a necessity. Jingle Nodes brought cuting edge P2P Technology to support platforms running more than 20 Million calls a day.

  • 2012-2014

    Pure IP Telecoms

    Fully IP based, Mobile Platforms providing services through Data Only SIM cards and XMPP Jingle. Live product launched in Spain and Switzerland.

  • 2015-2016

    Natural Languages of Things

    Mixing meta programming languages, XMPP and Internet of Things, Proventus is innovating how People and Things communicate.
